In this image titled "Cosmic Reef", the red giant nebula (NGC 2014) and its blue "neighbor" (NGC 2020) are located in the Large Magellanic Cloud, a satellite galaxy of the Milky Way. The Milky Way is located about 163,000 light-years from Earth. At the center of NGC 2014 is a series of bright stars, each 10-20 times more massive than the Sun. The image has been dubbed the "cosmic reef" because astronomers think the nebula resembles an undersea world.
